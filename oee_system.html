OEE Calculations
Formulas and live calculations used in this system
OEE Formula
OEE = Availability × Performance × Quality
The three pillars multiplied together give Overall Equipment Effectiveness.
Availability = Operating Time / Planned Production Time
Operating Time = PPT − Downtime (unplanned). PPT = Shift Length − Planned Breaks.
Performance = Total Count / (Ideal Rate × Operating Time)
Ideal Rate in bags/min × Operating Time in minutes = theoretical max output.
Quality = Good Pieces / Total Count
Good Pieces = Total Count − Rejected / Defective pieces.
Planned Production Time
PPT = Shift Length − Planned Breaks

Loss Calculations
Availability Loss = 1 − Availability
Performance Loss = Availability × (1 − Performance)
Quality Loss = Availability × Performance × (1 − Quality)
Total Loss = 1 − OEE

  sheet.appendRow(data.row);

}
